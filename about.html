WHO WE ARE

Our goal is to be the most trusted, leading, state-of-the-art Canadian elevator interior manufacturing company.

Premier Elevator started its operations in 1992 with the goal of delivering our innovative, quality services with the utmost care and integrity throughout the province of Ontario. With each year of service, we strive to maintain our position as a leader in elevator design and installation.

Our extensive client list includes original equipment manufacturers (OEMs), independent contractors, local property managers, national hospitality corporations, architects, and interior designers. Premier Elevator is widely recognized for our detailed craftsmanship matched with our first-class customer service.
